Fitness Studio Member Churn Prediction

Missed workouts. Fading motivation. Silent churn.

342,000 calories burned. 1.5 million steps. 861 miles on the treadmill. These personal metrics, achieved as an Orangetheory Fitness member, reinforce my belief in how data drives improvement. That passion fueled this four-part data science project aimed at identifying early signs of when members quietly stop engaging or cancel their membership (the process known as churn) and turning those signals into actionable retention strategies.

Built on a simulated real-world dataset, it mimics the lifecycle of fitness studio churn, tackling common data challenges and making the approach applicable to other subscription-based businesses like fintech or SaaS.

Results at a Glance

  • Churn Prediction: Engineered a model achieving 68% accuracy in forecasting member churn, providing an early warning system for timely, targeted retention efforts.
  • Actionable Strategic Insights: Identified engagement and membership tenure as the primary drivers of churn, equipping leadership with direct, data-driven levers to optimize retention strategies.
  • Efficient & Reliable Outreach: Delivered a model with 58% precision in flagging at-risk members, ensuring retention resources are focused efficiently on genuinely vulnerable accounts and minimizing wasted effort.

Churn costs U.S. companies an estimated $168 billion annually

Churn drains over $168 billion from U.S. companies each year (Forbes), hitting hardest in sectors like telecom, fintech, and SaaS. This project directly addresses that gap by using behavioral data to spot early risk signals, giving businesses a critical head start on retention. Beyond lost revenue, high churn increases customer acquisition costs, erodes brand loyalty, and disrupts forecasting. Yet, fewer than 1 in 4 companies have a formal retention strategy in place.

What's inside this project

Part 1 - ETL Pipeline
Cleans and transforms raw studio data, with the option to load into a Snowflake environment

Part 2 - Churn EDA
Explores engagement trends, class behavior, and tenure signals across member segments

Part 3 - Churn Prediction Modeling
Builds and compares logistic regression and random forest models

Part 4 - Next Steps
Translates model output into targeted retention tactics

Anticipate, Act, Retain

Ultimately, this work empowers operators to anticipate risk, act sooner, personalize outreach, and improve member retention without relying on guesswork.
